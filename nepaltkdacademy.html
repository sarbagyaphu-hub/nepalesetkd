Introduction

Nepalese Taekwondo Academy is a modern and disciplined training institution dedicated to promoting the spirit of taekwondo in Nepal. Since its establishment on November 30, 2014, the academy has continued to nurture athletes with technical excellence, moral values, physical confidence, and strong competitive vision.

About the Academy

A Place Where Skill, Discipline, and Respect Grow Together

The academy is built on a simple but powerful vision: to create not only medal-winning players, but also disciplined and confident individuals. Our training environment combines physical conditioning, technical taekwondo fundamentals, poomsae development, sparring preparation, and martial arts values that help students succeed inside and outside the training hall.

With a professional image, strong leadership, and affiliation to Nepal Taekwondo Association, Nepalese Taekwondo Academy represents dedication, continuity, and a genuine commitment to the growth of taekwondo.

Founder’s Message
“

Taekwondo Is More Than a Sport—It Is a Way of Building Character

Welcome to Nepalese Taekwondo Academy. Since the beginning of this academy, my dream has been to create a place where students can grow not only as fighters, but also as disciplined, respectful, and confident individuals.

Taekwondo teaches us to remain humble in victory, strong in challenge, and focused in our goals. Every athlete who enters the academy carries potential, and it is our responsibility to guide that potential with technical quality, moral values, and a positive environment.

I believe that with dedication, hard work, and right guidance, our players can rise to greater heights and proudly represent themselves, their academy, and their country. Thank you for being part of this journey.

— Ashok Khadka
Founder, Nepalese Taekwondo Academy
