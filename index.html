About the Academy

A Professional Home for Taekwondo Development

Nepalese Taekwondo Academy is associated with Nepal Taekwondo Association and has developed a reputation for quality coaching, athlete preparation, and meaningful taekwondo service in Kathmandu. The academy emphasizes both traditional values and modern training standards, creating an environment where students can grow in confidence, control, athletic ability, and competitive readiness.

Established: November 30, 2014
Location: Kathmandu, Nepal

Leadership and Technical Excellence

Senior Master Ashok Khadka

Founder and Chief • International Referee

Senior Master Ashok Khadka is the founder and chief of Nepalese Taekwondo Academy and a respected taekwondo figure known for technical depth, leadership, and international refereeing expertise. His experience spans kyorugi, poomsae, and para taekwondo refereeing, reflecting both versatility and a high level of professional understanding in the sport.

Kukkiwon Rank

Kukkiwon 6th Dan with strong technical foundation and long-term contribution to taekwondo development.

International Refereeing

Recognized experience as an international referee in kyorugi, poomsae, and para categories.

Association Affiliation

Associated with Nepal Taekwondo Association and committed to strengthening taekwondo standards.
